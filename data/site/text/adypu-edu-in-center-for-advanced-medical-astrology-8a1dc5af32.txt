Medical Astrology

Your Journey into the Healing Through the Stars: Medical Astrology Course

Discover the ancient art of medical astrology, where celestial bodies influence health and wellness. Our comprehensive course bridges astrology and medicine, providing insights into how planetary positions can reflect physical and emotional well-being. Designed for both astrology enthusiasts and health professionals, this course offers a deep understanding of astrology’s role in healing. Open to anyone with an interest, regardless of background, this program will equip you with the tools to interpret health patterns through the stars.

Is medical astrology a recognized medical practice? No, medical astrology is a complementary practice that offers insights based on astrology but should not replace professional medical advice.

Do I need a background in healthcare to enroll? No prior healthcare knowledge is required; the course is open to all astrology enthusiasts.

Will I learn how to predict specific health issues? The course will teach you how to interpret health-related astrological patterns, but it is not a diagnostic tool.

Will study material be provided? Yes, you will be provided with a PDF file containing the study material.
