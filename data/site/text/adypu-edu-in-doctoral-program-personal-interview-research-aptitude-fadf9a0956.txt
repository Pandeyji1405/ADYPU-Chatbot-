Interview / viva-voce

An interview / viva-voce to be organized by the ADYPU when the candidates are required to present their research interest / area through a presentation before a duly constituted Doctoral Research Committee.

The Ph.D Aspirant is expected to prepare and submit the PowerPoint and MS Word Document which highlights the following aspects,

Personal Introduction & Professional Achievements.

The Aspirations for Pursuing Ph.D Program (200-300 words)

The PPT should be of 15 minutes. It should have a minimum of 15-20 slides.

Format for Proposed Research Idea

The candidates of ADYPU Ph.D. Program are expected to submit the research proposal in the prescribed format mentioned below:

Name of the Applicant

Specialization

Title: Authors should attempt at their best to focus the titles of their research projects to be in line with national needs and the research themes of the university.

Abstract: A short summary (no more than 600 words) of your research proposal and its significance.The students are expected to cite references as well as bibliography in the Harvard Business Review (HBR) Referencing style.

The Research Proposal should have brief description of the research plan focusing on

Research Background

Significance of the Research

Research Methodology

Benefit/Effect of the Result obtained from
