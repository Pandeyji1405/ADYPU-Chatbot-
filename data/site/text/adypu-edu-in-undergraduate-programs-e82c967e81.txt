Welcome to the diverse and enriching world of undergraduate courses at ADYPU. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously crafted to provide students with a foundation of knowledge, critical thinking skills, and practical experience that prepares them for success in their chosen fields. We offer a wide array of undergraduate courses spanning various disciplines, each designed to foster intellectual curiosity and promote a holistic approach to learning.

Whether you are passionate about engineering, business, humanities, or the sciences, our undergraduate programs are tailored to meet the evolving demands of the professional landscape.

Join us on a journey of academic exploration, where dedicated faculty, innovative curriculum, and a vibrant campus community come together to shape the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and change-makers. Explore our undergraduate courses and embark on a path that aligns with your aspirations and unlocks a world of opportunities.

Courses Offered

Welcome to the realm of innovation and engineering excellence at the School of Engineering. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously crafted to provide students with a strong foundation in engineering principles necessary to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology and innovation.

Discover the world of undergraduate courses at the School of Management, where education meets innovation and business acumen. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously designed to provide students with a solid foundation in management necessary to excel in the ever-evolving business landscape.

Creativity knows no bounds at the School of Design. Our undergraduate programs are carefully designed to foster artistic talent, critical thinking, and innovation. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of designers and visual storytellers.

The world of undergraduate courses at the School of Law, a gateway to the fascinating and dynamic realm of legal studies. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously designed to provide students with a solid foundation in law required for success in the complex world of legal practice.

Step into the world of hospitality and excellence at the School of Hotel Management, where education meets the art of hospitality. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously designed to prepare students for careers in the ever-evolving hospitality industry.

The diverse and intellectually stimulating world of undergraduate courses at the School of Liberal Arts is a space where the pursuit of knowledge knows no boundaries. Our undergraduate programs are thoughtfully designed to encouraging students to explore, analyze, and engage with complex issues from diverse perspectives.

School of Film & Media

Step into the captivating world of undergraduate courses at the School of Film and Media, where storytelling and creativity merge into a dynamic learning experience. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously crafted to nurture the next generation of filmmakers, media professionals, and visual storytellers.

Creativity and innovation converge to shape the architects of tomorrow. Our undergraduate programs are meticulously designed to provide students with a strong foundation in architectural principles and hands-on experiences, preparing them for the challenges of the ever-evolving field of architecture.

Why Study at ADYPU

Studying at ADYPU (Ajeenkya DY Patil University) offers a unique blend of academic excellence and real-world exposure. With cutting-edge programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a focus on experiential learning, ADYPU provides students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in today’s dynamic job market. The university’s commitment to innovation, industry partnerships, and personalized mentorship makes it a compelling choice for those seeking a forward looking, career-focused education.
