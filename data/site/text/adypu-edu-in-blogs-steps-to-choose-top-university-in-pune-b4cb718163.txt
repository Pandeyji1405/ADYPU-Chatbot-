Steps to Choose Top University in Pune

October 10, 2019

With over 100 universities, colleges and limitless study options on offer, Pune’s higher education experience is as unique as you are.

There are dedicated universities and colleges, world-renowned historic institutions, modern innovative facilities and pioneering and progressive research centers in locations all over the city, offering you the chance to tailor your learning to your aspirations and career goals. There are also universities and colleges that have a very strong focus upon a specific subject area, such as business, art, and design, music or engineering. These institutions offer highly specialized education in your chosen field and you will be surrounded by like-minded students – great for focused development, future contacts as well as making friends.

Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU), one of the best universities in Pune known for its leading-edge technology and high quality education experience. With courses led by our on-campus faculty, hands-on experiences with real-world organizations, and opportunities to engage with classmates, the ADYPU offers the same esteemed degrees, just in a flexible and convenient format.

Here are the most important criteria to consider when choosing your future university to study in Pune:

Accreditations and ranking

One of the biggest factors in deciding which university to go to – maybe even the biggest factor – is how each university performs in the league tables. It can be well worth your time to carefully study how a university ranks in different subjects, as well as its overall ranking.

ADYPU has a solid framework of accreditations and ranking. The #CoolestUniversity and its curriculum has the backing of Accreditation Board Of Engineering & Technology (ABET, USA), Computing Science Accreditation Board, USA (CSAB, USA), subject benchmark statements by the Quality Assurance Agency (QAA), UK, and many more.

Programs & Pedagogy

Find the right course, at the right university or college, and you will be inspired to succeed. Learn how you will be taught and assessed, like whether a course has a lot of exams, essays, or group work, and also consider the course content.

ADYPU is one of the top most universities in India. Each year we welcome a large community of students onto a wide range of courses; including Business, Computing, Design, Engineering, Law and many more. It is here where you’ll find a perfect blend of merging technology with traditional wisdom and existing knowledge with revolutionary thinking.

University facilities University facilities play a pivotal role in improving the work efficiency of both teachers and students. The university must invest in comfortable, attractive, safe and technology-enabled spaces teaching and learning spaces. ADYPU has the perfect environment in which you can learn, develop your skills and above all have a great time. Relax with friends in halls, catch a play, or get involved in sports clubs and societies – there’s plenty to entertain you on campus. Transform your future by studying at a world-leading and the Coolest University.

Teaching & Learning

By pedagogy, we mean the dynamic relationship between learning, teaching and culture. Imparting appropriate and transferable practical skills is essential to the learning strategy of the program.

At ADYPU, the Analytical and problem solving skills are developed using a range of appropriate ‘real’ and ‘theoretical’ case studies and problem / task-based learning. Our teaching and learning processes promote research, experimentation, analysis, evaluation, and self-criticism – all of which assist in arriving at the best possible solutions to a given academic or industrial challenge.

T-Shaped Professional

What is a T-shaped professional? While the concept of T-shaped skills is not so new, it is now more important than ever.

ADYPU commits to develop its students as a T-Shaped Professional. Employers and educators have started to think more broadly about what it means to graduate with the right skills to be ready for a job.

Social atmosphere

More than being an institution of higher learning; it is a community where people of diverse cultures and nationalities live, work, and play — connected by their similarities and enriched by their differences.

Life at ADYPU is eclectic. You’ll find unparalleled academic options, alongside a multitude of different social, cultural, recreational and wellness initiatives. Each campus has a team dedicated to providing the best supports, opportunities and resources for students to reach their full potential.

All in all, ADYPU is about more than an education. The university experience gives you the knowledge, skills, confidence and experience to help you make your world better. You’ll have the opportunity to broaden your horizons by meeting new people from all over the world, facing new challenges and making use of the huge range of new activities, clubs and societies on offer. You may even be able to take part of your course overseas and learn a new language and culture.

If you choose to graduate from ADYPU, you’ll prepared for the changing landscape of the 21st century and your time at the top university of Pune will provide you with challenges and experiences which will shape your future.
