B.Sc. in Forensic Science

Undergraduate Programmes

Uncover the secrets behind the science of solving crimes! The B.Sc in Forensic Science at ADYPU equips students with scientific techniques and investigative skills to aid criminal justice. Dive into a thrilling world of fingerprints, DNA, toxicology, and crime scene analysis.

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

10+2 or equivalent from any recognized board, with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.

Program Objectives

To develop strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.

To provide advanced lab-based training in forensic methods.

To integrate science, law, and technology for real-world case analysis.

To build professional skills for careers in investigation, research, and consulting.

How You Will Be Taught

Hands-on Training: State-of-the-art forensic labs and crime scene simulations.

Interactive Learning: Guest lectures by police officers, legal experts, and forensic scientists.

Industry Exposure: Case studies, internships, and live investigations with forensic labs & legal agencies.

Mentorship: Guidance from expert faculty and industry professionals.

Course Structure Highlights

Core Subjects: Forensic Chemistry, Forensic Biology, Criminalistics, Forensic Psychology, Digital & Cyber Forensics.

Electives: DNA Profiling, Wildlife Forensics, Fingerprint Analysis.

Practical Training: Lab experiments, mock trials, internships, field projects.
