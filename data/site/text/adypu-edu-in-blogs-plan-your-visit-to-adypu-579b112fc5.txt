Plan your Visit to ADYPU

October 10, 2019

College visits are probably the most fun part of the college application process. When it comes to researching potential colleges, there’s no better substitute for figuring out if a campus will feel like home. No website, guidebook, or testimonial will give you a better feel for a college than you’ll get by seeing it for yourself.

And while considering to study at Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU), Lohegaon, Pune, the best way to get a true sense of the #CoolestUniversity experience is by visiting.

Why Visit ADYPU Campus?

ADYPU boasts a beautiful urban campus with wide open green spaces, vibrant student culture, and unique architecture. We would argue it’s one of the best campuses in the city but don’t just take our word for it. Come visit us!

Studying with us, we will challenge and inspire you. You will receive the best academic preparation to achieve in your chosen discipline and equip you with knowledge and skills to make a real difference.

Discover our campus life

We offer a range of opportunities, events and activities to socially and culturally enrich your wellness and success within and outside the classroom.

University accommodation

ADYPU understands that accommodation plays an important role in setting the foundation for academic success. Here, students have access to an impressive portfolio of University-managed student accommodation options, made available specifically to support a successful transition into University life.

Our vibrant campus

There’s always plenty to see and do while you’re studying at our campus. Throughout the year at the many events and activities we host, you’ll love being part of our campus community.

Sport, clubs and fitness

There are plenty of ways to stay fit and healthy while you study with us. Find your crowd by signing up to one of our many clubs or visit one of our Fitness Hubs.

Security on campus

Security is available 24 hours 7 days per week and is conveniently located on the campus. The University provides a range of services to assist in staff and student safety, including the university Security Service, but we rely on the whole campus community to work together to support safety on campus.

When the above reasons give you enough motivation, we invite you to visit our campus on any working day and discover more about ADYPU. Talk to your academic advisors, meet the faculty, take a tour of the campus, see student work and convince yourself why you should choose ADYPU to be your next destination.

Visiting us is quite easy. Just give us a buzz before you arrive and we’d happily show you around our spectacular campus.

How to reach the campus

Following a Google Map is recommended.

Ajeenkya DY Patil University is around 19 min (6.3 km) via Porwal Rd & 25 min (9.0 km) via DY Patil College Rd.

If you’re at Pune station, you can always book a cab and reach our campus within an hour’s time.

Our address: Charoli Bk.via Lohegaon, District Pune – 412 105, Maharashtra, India

Whom to contact?

Before you reach the college, premises come prepared with a few questions to ask. We would love to answer them. Upon your arrival, you can have a word with our academic advisors, meet the faculty members and students and get to know us better.

You’re welcome any day from Monday to Friday between 11 AM – 4.30 PM. Please call 020 – 67077942 / 43 to schedule the date and time of your visit.

In case, if you are unable to make it due to any reason, feel free to contact us. We can help you in the best possible way.

For any further query, you can mail us at info@adypu.edu.in

When you step into our glorious campus and start your academic journey, you’re gaining a passport to a successful life and career. Our pioneering taught courses draw upon our world-leading research and our strong links to the global industry. You’ll quickly develop skills; knowledge and experience that will make employers sit up and listen.

We’d like you to be a part of the ADYPU family!
