B.Sc. (Hons.) Anaesthesia & Operation Theatre Technology (A&OTT)

The B. Sc. Hons. in Anaesthesia & Operation Theatre Technology is a comprehensive four-year program designed to prepare students to become skilled professionals in perioperative and anaesthesia care. The course combines classroom learning, hands-on training in simulation labs, and clinical exposure to operating theatres. Students gain expertise in assisting anaesthesiologists, managing OT equipment, and ensuring smooth surgical workflows. Graduates are equipped to work in hospitals, surgical centers, and emergency care units.

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility:

Candidate should have passed Senior Secondary Examination (10 +2) in science stream from any recognized Board in PCB

Why B.Sc. (Hons.) in AOTT?

This program at Ajeenkya DY Patil University offers a blend of technical and practical knowledge, fostering advanced problem-solving skills and independent learning. The curriculum is designed to meet global standards in surgical care, making graduates highly sought after in healthcare institutions.
