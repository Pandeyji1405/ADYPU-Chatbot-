Programs Offered

The Master of Science (M.Sc.) programs at Ajeenkya DY Patil University’s School of Science are designed for inquisitive minds ready to advance their scientific knowledge and drive innovation in specialized fields. With a strong emphasis on research, practical applications, and interdisciplinary learning, the M.Sc. the curriculum bridges academic theory with real-world problem-solving.

Students benefit from expert faculty guidance, access to advanced laboratory infrastructure, and exposure to global scientific trends. Our postgraduate programs are structured to produce skilled scientists, researchers, and professionals who can contribute meaningfully to academia, industry, and technology-driven sectors.
