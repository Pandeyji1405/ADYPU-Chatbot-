How to Choose the Right Robotics and Automation College in India: A Complete Guide

July 15, 2025

For numerous learners, selecting a trusted institute to pursue robotics and mechanisation can be confusing. You should look for a college that will teach you skills, assist you with employment and offer hands-on labs. In this blog, you will find out how to locate the right institute for robotics and automation in India. You will also gain insights about the faculty, industry ties, practical labs, etc. So, let’s begin.

Finding A College for Robotics and Automation: How to Do So?

When you’re looking for a reputable robotics and automation colleges in India, here are some of the most effective ways to locate one:

1. Check the Curriculum

Look at the course list carefully. A good program starts with the basics, which consist of mathematics, programming, electronics, etc. After that, it moves to all the main topics, such as advanced robotics, automatic, control and even mechanics. For instance, the B.Tech in Robotics and Automation programme from ADYPU teaches all these topics clearly. In programmes like robotics programming, ML, PLC and IoT, you need to learn actual-world usage and even theory.

2. Look for Hands-On Labs

Practical labs are vital. A strong robotics program has well-stocked labs with modern gear. ADYPU mentions labs like a drone tech lab, an industrial automation lab by Schneider Electric, and a dedicated robotics lab. Be sure to inspect for 3D printing, CAD/CAM, and robot power labs. Hands-on assignment allows you to understand better, create your resume, and build conviction.

3. Industry Partnerships & Certifications

Good automation and robotics colleges in India partner with companies. ADYPU works with big names like ABB, Honeywell, and Schneider Electric. These tie-ups help you get internships, certification programs, and real projects. ADYPU also offers free certifications in AWS, Blue Prism (automated process mechanisation), Palo Alto, Microchip, and Schneider Electric. These add-ons boost your learning and job chances.

4. Projects & Real-World Experience

See if the program includes projects at every year level. ADYPU uses a year-by-year project model: design tasks in the 1st year, course assignments in the 2nd, industrial assignments in the 3rd, and analysis in the 4th. This incremental build-up lets you apply what you understand in real surroundings. It also shows possible employers that you can address actual challenges.

5. Faculty Quality

Well-trained teachers matter. You need professors with strong degrees and authentic experience. ADYPU’s engineering academy has faculty experienced in robotics, mechanisation, AI, and even mechatronics. They train students to use the latest tools and provide the best industry practices via guest lectures and even workshops.

6. Global Exposure

A good college gives you global exposure. ADYPU associates itself with top universities like Coventry, the University of South Carolina, Tel Aviv University, and Strate School. These let you go overseas, attend international programmes, and learn from different cultures, which is excellent for growth and your resume.

7. Entrance Needs & Expenses

Check what scores and entry exams are needed. At ADYPU, B.Tech needs 45% in Physics, Chemistry & Maths, with the entrance exam ADYPU AIEAT or JEE/MH-CET. Fee-wise, the total B. Tech cost is around ₹10 lakhs to ₹10 lakhs.

8. Scholarships & Support

See what scholarships are available. ADYPU gives many scholarships: Ajeenkya Genius, Pune Pride, Beti Padhao, sports, resilience, sibling, and others. Scholarships help reduce costs and show that the college cares about its students.

9. Placements & Internship Help

Good colleges help you find job placements and internships. ADYPU’s School of Engineering has a strong placement cell. They get industry connections, workshops, interview training, and mentorship. Reviews say robotics grads from ADYPU have been placed in top companies like Tata and Wipro. Highest packages went up to ₹53 LPA, and the average was around ₹4 to 12 LPA.

10. Campus & Student Life

A vibrant campus and clubs make college fun and productive. ADYPU has an IEEE student branch and robotics society for collaboration, events, hackathons, and workshops. Also, they host value-added programs like 30‑hour Arduino-based robotics. These experiences matter for learning and networking.

11. Accreditation & Recognition

Ensure that the best colleges in India for robotics and automation are approved by the UGC/AICTE. ADYPU is a UGC‑approved private university, and its engineering programs are UGC-approved. The university has won awards like “Leading University in Innovation” and “Most Promising Engineering Institute” in 2021. These boost its credibility.

12. Entry-Level vs Advanced Degrees

If you want to go deeper, check if they have M.Tech. ADYPU offers M.Tech in Robotics & Automation for B.Tech grads with 50% marks. It’s approved by UGC, with an April deadline each year. So you can continue in the same place for advanced studies.Choosing the best colleges to study robotics in India indicates looking for strong programmes, laboratories, assignments, industry associations, and employment assistance. ADYPU offers all this in a smooth, user-friendly way. Their B.Tech program covers core and advanced topics, includes top-tier labs, certifications, real projects, and global ties. They also present scholarships and a reliable placement history. If you’re looking for a future-focused and well-rounded engineering programme, ADYPU in Pune stands out as a smart option.
