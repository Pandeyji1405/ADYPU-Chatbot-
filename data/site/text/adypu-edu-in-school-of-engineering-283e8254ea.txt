Core features

Innoventive Engineering

Our curriculum integrates Innoventive Engineering, emphasizing design thinking, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial skills. It engages students in authentic problem-solving experiences, fostering social collaboration and introducing innovative product development methods

Distinguished Faculty

At ADYPU, we take pride in our accomplished faculty who are not only experts in their respective fields but are also passionate mentors. They inspire and guide our students, fostering a culture of curiosity and critical thinking.

Hi-Tech Laboratories

Our world-class infrastructure and advanced laboratories provide students with hands-on experience and a conducive environment for research and innovation. From well-equipped workshops to cutting-edge research centers, we offer the resources necessary for academic and professional growth.

Industry Collaboration

We maintain strong partnerships with leading industries and organizations, ensuring that our students have access to internships, real-world projects, and opportunities for networking. This collaboration bridges the gap between academia and industry, preparing our graduates for the challenges of the professional world.

Start-Up Culture

We encourage an entrepreneurial spirit and innovation in our students. Through incubators, hackathons, and entrepreneurial programs, we empower students to transform their ideas into practical solutions that address real-world problems.

Global Immersion

ADYPU School of Engineering recognizes the importance of a global perspective in engineering. We offer international exchange programs, collaboration with foreign universities, and an inclusive, diverse campus environment that broadens students' horizons and prepares them for a global career.

The School of Engineering boasts a remarkable track record when it comes to placing its graduates in prestigious positions. With consistently high placement rates, our graduates find themselves in diverse career opportunities across various engineering specializations, from software engineering, aerospace and electrical,. Our career development services ensure that students are well-prepared for job placements, while our dedicated partnerships with industry leaders offer unique opportunities for internships and employment. Graduates have gone on to secure exciting roles at companies such as KPIT, Accenture, Bosch, Royal Enfield, and Tata Consultancy Services.
