Center for Advanced Indian Sciences

Center of Advanced Indian Studies (CAIS) is a vibrant hub committed to rejuvenating India’s splendid cultural legacy. Our commitment goes beyond traditional education; we offer courses and have created a platform for research, providing a space for innovative exploration and understanding of India’s ancient system. Our approach is unique, blending ancient insights with modern knowledge, ensuring a holistic and enriching educational experience.

We are bridging the gap between the past and the future, discovering the connection to our cultural roots while embracing the limitless possibilities of advanced sciences. This field of study paves the door for new career paths and employability.

Join us as we lead the way in providing courses in Astronomy and Astrophysics, extending an invitation to all space enthusiasts. Dive into the world of cosmic wonders while exploring the fusion of ancient Indian wisdom with scientific knowledge.
