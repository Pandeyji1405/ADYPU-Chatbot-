Welcome Back, Alumni!

Welcome to your dedicated portal within the Ajeenkya D Y Patil University community! This space is designed to reconnect you with your alma mater, fellow graduates, and the vibrant future of ADYPU.

As an alumni you’re more than just a graduate – you’re an ambassador for our legacy of excellence. We invite you to explore the opportunities that await you here:

Rekindle old friendships and build new connections: Network with fellow alumni across industries and continents, fostering lifelong bonds and professional support.

Stay informed and engaged: Keep up-to-date on the latest university news, events, and achievements, and share your own success stories.

Give back to the community: Mentor current students, offer career guidance, or contribute to scholarships, shaping the next generation of ADYPU leaders.

Access exclusive resources: Enjoy library access, career support services, and special alumni discounts.

Together, we can continue to build the legacy of Ajeenkya D Y Patil University. Join us, explore, and reconnect!

Being an ADYPU graduate opens doors to the Alumni Community

Lifelong Learning

Learning will not stop once you graduate from ADYPU. Our dedication to your academic life is a continuous journey with a range of flexible study further alternatives.

From the start of your ADYPU life, our career placement and counseling teams help you achieve the best in your future by the end of your degree and beyond.

Alumni Events and Reunions

No matter which corner of the world you are in, you can stay connected with the ADYPU alumni community with regular events and reunions.

ADYPU offers ample resources and facilities to help you fine tune your resume, expand your network, and give back to the university.

Library Access

As an ADYPU Alumni, you can borrow books from the library, access online library resources, or make full use of the library infrastructure.

Startup Programs and Support

As an ADYPU Alumni, you can avail support for your entrepreneurial ideas, get guidance from professors, and expand skills to accelerate career opportunities.

Alumni Meet

An annual gathering of Ajeenkya D Y Patil University (ADYPU) graduates, the Alumni Meet is a vibrant celebration of shared memories and achievements. Held at the picturesque Pune campus, it brings together alumni from diverse fields and years, fostering a strong sense of community. Expect nostalgic campus tours, inspiring talks, lively entertainment, and ample opportunities to reconnect with old friends and professors. It’s a chance to reminisce, reignite your Adypu spirit, and network with a powerful alumni network!

What Makes Alumni Meets Unique

ADYPU alumni meets aren’t your typical gatherings. They blend vibrant reunions with a spirit of innovation and community building. Here’s what sets them apart:

Industry Leaders & Mentorship: Expect to connect with successful alumni across diverse fields, fostering professional growth and guidance.

Global Network: Mingle with graduates from worldwide, expanding your professional circle and potential collaborations.

Lifelong Learning: Engage in workshops, talks, and discussions, staying updated on industry trends and acquiring new skills.

Celebratory Spirit: Rejoice in shared memories, relive campus moments, and strengthen lifelong friendships

Giving Back: Contribute to the university’s growth through volunteering, scholarships, or mentorship programs.

Entrepreneurial Hub: Network with fellow alumni entrepreneurs, fostering business partnerships and joint ventures.
