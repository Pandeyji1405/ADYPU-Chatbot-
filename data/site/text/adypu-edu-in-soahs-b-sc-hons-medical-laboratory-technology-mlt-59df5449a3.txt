B.Sc. (Hons.) Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT)

The B. Sc. Hons. in Medical Lab Technology program equips students with the knowledge and technical skills to perform essential diagnostic procedures that form the backbone of effective healthcare. This four-year program combines foundational sciences with advanced laboratory techniques, enabling students to specialize in clinical chemistry, microbiology, haematology, and more. The curriculum integrates classroom learning with laboratory training, ensuring graduates are prepared to meet the demands of diagnostic centers, hospitals, and research institutions.

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility:

Candidate should have passed Senior Secondary Examination (10 +2) in science stream from any recognized Board.

Why B.Sc. (Hons.) in MLT?

The MLT program at Ajeenkya DY Patil University focuses on creating professionals with advanced diagnostic capabilities and critical thinking skills. The program emphasizes research-based learning and hands-on practice, preparing students to meet global healthcare challenges with confidence.
