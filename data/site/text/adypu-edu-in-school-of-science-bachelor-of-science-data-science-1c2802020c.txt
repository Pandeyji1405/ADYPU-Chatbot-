B.Sc. Data Science

Undergraduate Programmes

Explore the world of data with a B.Sc. in Data Science—an interdisciplinary program that blends statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge. This course equips students with the skills to collect, process, and interpret data to solve real-world problems. Learn how to turn raw data into actionable insights and support informed decision-making across industries.

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

10+2 or equivalent from any recognized board, with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.

Program Overview

The B.Sc. Data Science program offers a comprehensive foundation in data analysis, statistics, machine learning, and programming. Students will gain hands-on experience with tools and technologies such as Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and more, enabling them to explore large datasets, build predictive models, and solve real-world problems. The curriculum is curated to balance theoretical knowledge with practical application, fostering analytical thinking and innovation.

Program Objectives

The B.Sc. Data Science program aims to:

Provide a strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, and computer programming.

Develop analytical and problem-solving skills through real-world data applications.

Equip students with the ability to extract, analyze, and interpret large datasets using modern tools and technologies.

Foster critical thinking, data-driven decision-making, and ethical considerations in data handling.

Prepare students for advanced studies or careers in data science, analytics, artificial intelligence, and related fields.

How You Will Be Taught

Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Developed with input from data science professionals and industry experts.

Hands-On Learning: Through case studies, labs, live projects, and internships.

Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced educators and data science practitioners.

Workshops & Seminars: Regular sessions with guest speakers from top tech companies and research organizations.

Capstone Project: Apply your knowledge in a final-year project that solves a real-world data challenge.
