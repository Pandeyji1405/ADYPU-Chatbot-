Basic Astrology

Your Journey into the Unlock the Mysteries of the Stars: Basic Astrology Course

Delve into the fascinating world of astrology with our 6-month online program designed for beginners. Whether you’re curious about your birth chart or seeking to understand cosmic patterns, this course offers a solid foundation in astrology. With no prior experience required, our program is open to anyone who has completed 12th from any stream. Start your celestial journey and discover the wisdom hidden in the stars.

Duration: 1 Year

Eligibility :

12th from any stream (Arts/Commerce/Science)

Mode : Online

Program Highlights

Expert guidance from professional astrologers

In-depth study of planetary influences and birth charts

Flexible learning schedule with self-paced modules

Access to exclusive resources and astrology tools

What Will You Explore?:

Basics of astrological signs, planets, and houses

Birth chart analysis

Understanding planetary transits

Astrology and its relation to personality, career, and relationships

Do I need prior knowledge of astrology? No, the course is designed for beginners with no prior experience.

Can I pursue this course alongside my job or studies? Yes, the online mode and flexible schedule allow you to balance it with other commitments.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion? Yes, you’ll receive a certificate of completion once all course requirements are met.

Will study material be provided? Yes, you will be provided with a PDF file containing the study material.
