B.Sc. (Hons.) Respiratory Technology (RT)

The ADYPU School of Health & Allied Science offers a rigorous four-year undergraduate B. Sc. Hons. program in Respiratory Therapy, designed to address the growing demand for skilled professionals in pulmonary care. This program bridges theoretical knowledge with hands-on clinical exposure, equipping students to manage respiratory illnesses and provide critical care support. Students are trained in advanced respiratory diagnostics, therapeutic procedures, and ventilator management, alongside developing problem-solving and analytical skills. Graduates are prepared to excel in hospitals, critical care units, diagnostic centers, and research settings, meeting the ever-evolving challenges of respiratory healthcare.

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility:

Candidate should have passed Senior Secondary Examination (10 +2) in science stream from any recognized Board in PCB

Advanced Skills: Training includes managing ventilators, respiratory diagnostics, and therapeutic procedures.

Global Opportunities: Opportunities exist in international healthcare markets, especially with growing respiratory healthcare needs.

Path to Specialization: Graduates can pursue advanced studies in pulmonology, critical care, or healthcare management.
