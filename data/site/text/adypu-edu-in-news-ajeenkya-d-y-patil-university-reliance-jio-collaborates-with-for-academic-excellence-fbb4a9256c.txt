Ajeenkya D Y Patil University Reliance Jio Collaborates with for Academic Excellence

January 21, 2025

Reliance Jio has joined hands with Ajeenkya D Y Patil University (ADYPU) to enhance academic excellence and bridge the gap between industry and academia. This collaboration aims to introduce cutting-edge advancements in telecommunications and electronics while supporting curriculum development, student growth, and multidisciplinary learning.

Ajeenkya D Y Patil University (ADYPU) recently had the privilege of hosting Shri Sunil Gosavi, Vice President of Reliance Jio, along with his colleagues, Mr. Abbas Kadari and Mr. Nishant. During their visit, they engaged in an insightful discussion with Vice-Chancellor Dr. Rakesh Kumar Jain, other Deans, and senior faculty members, highlighting several key areas of collaboration.

Such as Reliance Jio executives, led by Mr. Gosavi, will serve as resource persons in telecommunications and management.

They will contribute to curriculum development, ensuring that the courses align with the latest industry trends.

The collaboration supports overall student development, including skill enhancement and career readiness.

Assistance with admissions to attract top-tier students to ADYPU.

As an alumnus of ADYPU, Mr. Sunil Gosavi expressed his enthusiasm for contributing to the university’s mission of providing multidisciplinary education. With over 34 years of professional experience in the telecom sector, Mr. Gosavi emphasized the importance of equipping engineering students with knowledge beyond their core domains, including law, management, and computer skills.

“ADYPU is an innovative university focusing on equipping students with multidisciplinary knowledge, which is essential in today’s competitive world,” stated Mr. Gosavi. He commended ADYPU for its forward-thinking approach and expressed his willingness to support the university’s initiatives by leveraging his extensive expertise and professional network.

The Vice-Chancellor, Dr. Rakesh Kumar Jain, welcomed the partnership, stating, “This association with Reliance Jio aligns with our vision to provide students with industry-relevant education. It will pave the way for new opportunities and foster innovation.”

Dr. Lalit Kumar Wadhava, Dean of Innovation and Rankings stressed the importance of such collaboration and invited the Reliance team to be part of the development of the education ecosystem.

Reliance Jio and ADYPU’s collaboration promises to create a dynamic ecosystem for learning, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the future.

This collaboration marks a significant step in bridging the gap between academia and industry. Reliance Jio’s partnership with ADYPU is set to enhance the knowledge and skills of students and faculty, particularly in the rapidly evolving fields of telecommunications and electronics.
