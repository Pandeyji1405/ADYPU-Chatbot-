Eligibility and Application Flexibility

The top MBA colleges in Maharashtra appeal to candidates coming from a variety of different undergraduate backgrounds of the undergraduates, thus the pool of talents and perspectives is diverse and rich. ADYPU permits graduates (10+2+3 or 10+2+4) of any stream with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories), and also allows final year students to apply. The university admits the scores of several national-level entrance exams, and also the university may have its own entrance exam, AICET, so the candidates have the freedom and convenience to choose.

Fee Transparency and Security

One of the greatest characteristics of the best MBA colleges in Maharashtra is the well-defined and secure means of fee payment. In ADYPU, the exam registration fee is Rs. 1,500, and it is non-refundable; moreover, all fee payments are to be made directly to the college account. Students and parents are advised not to transact with third-party agents if they want to ensure complete security and transparency. In case of cancellation of provisional admissions, a refund will be made within 10 days of the commencement date; hence, by this, ADYPU is reaffirming its commitment to the welfare of the students.
