School of AlliedHealth Sciences

The School of Allied Health Sciences (SAHS) at Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is dedicated to cultivating proficient healthcare professionals equipped to meet the dynamic needs of the medical sector. Our comprehensive curriculum encompasses a range of undergraduate programs, meticulously designed to blend theoretical knowledge with practical application. Students benefit from state-of-the-art laboratories and simulation centers, ensuring hands-on experience with the latest medical technologies.

Our esteemed faculty comprises seasoned academicians and industry practitioners who provide personalized mentorship, fostering both academic and professional growth. Beyond the classroom, SAHS emphasizes interdisciplinary collaboration, encouraging students to engage in research and community outreach initiatives. This holistic approach ensures our graduates are not only skilled clinicians but also compassionate caregivers and innovative thinkers, ready to excel in diverse healthcare environments.

The National Moot Court Competition is a prestigious event in the legal realm that brings together law students from across the country to participate in simulated court proceedings. This competition serves as a platform for aspiring lawyers to showcase their advocacy skills, legal knowledge, and ability to argue cases effectively before a panel of judges. Organized by the Law School of ADYPU, the National Moot Court Competition typically involves teams of law students preparing written briefs and presenting oral arguments on hypothetical legal cases. These cases are often based on real-life legal issues, providing participants with an opportunity to engage in rigorous legal research, analysis, and argumentation.

Career Pathways inAllied Health Sciences

Graduates from School of Allied Health Sciences can explore a multitude of career opportunities across various healthcare domains, including:

Clinical and Diagnostic Services – Work in hospitals, laboratories, and specialized healthcare centers.

Rehabilitation and Therapeutic Sciences – Engage in physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy.

Healthcare Management and Administration – Take up roles in hospital administration, healthcare consulting, and public health organizations.

Research and Development – Contribute to medical advancements through scientific research and innovation.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with an emphasis on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
