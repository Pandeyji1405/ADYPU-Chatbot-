B.Sc. Computer Science

Undergraduate Programmes

Dive into the digital realm with a B.Sc. in Computer Science—an industry-relevant program that integrates programming, algorithms, systems, and emerging technologies. This course empowers students to design, develop, and optimize computing solutions for complex problems. Gain the technical expertise and problem-solving skills needed to drive innovation in a tech-driven world.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility:

10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.

Program Overview

The B.Sc. Computer Science program offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers core areas such as algorithms, software development, artificial intelligence, database management, web technologies, and cybersecurity. Students will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills essential for the dynamic field of computer science. Emphasis is also placed on developing problem-solving abilities, logical thinking, and technical proficiency in multiple programming languages.

Project Objectives

To equip students with the ability to design and develop efficient software solutions.

To cultivate logical thinking, analytical problem-solving, and algorithmic skills.

To enhance understanding of real-world applications of computer science through practical projects.

To promote innovation and research-oriented thinking in areas like AI, cybersecurity, and data analytics.

To prepare students for global IT industry demands and entrepreneurial ventures.

How You Will Be Taught

Experiential Learning: Hands-on training through lab sessions, coding challenges, and real-time projects.

Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Regular updates based on industry trends and technological advancements.

Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced professionals and researchers in the field.

Workshops & Seminars: Regular tech talks, hackathons, and seminars to bridge academic learning with industry insights.

Internships: Opportunities for industry internships to gain practical exposure and professional experience.
