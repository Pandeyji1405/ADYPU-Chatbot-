Alumni Meet 2024

Alumni Meet 2024 – A Heartwarming Reunion at ADYPU

The Alumni Meet 2024, organized by the ADYPU Alumni Association, was held on 1st March 2024 at the Main Auditorium, Dental Building. This memorable evening served as a joyful homecoming for alumni from all batches, bringing together memories, connections, and inspiration.

The event began with a warm welcome by Janhavi Gandhe, Vice-President of the Student Council, followed by the ceremonial Saraswati Vandana and Diya lighting led by Hon’ble Vice Chancellor Hridaysh Deshpande. His inspiring address emphasized the university’s ongoing support for its alumni and the importance of staying connected through the Alumni Society.

Ms. Priya Abraham, Head of Student Services, highlighted the value of continuous alumni interaction in fostering mutual growth and networking. Heartfelt speeches from notable alumni shared their personal journeys and successes, reinforcing the strong bond they share with ADYPU.

The event also featured an Awards Ceremony:

Excelsior Award – Akshay Banker, Jr. Research Scientist, IIT Kanpur

Vice Chancellor’s Excellency Award – Aditya Bhandari, Cybersecurity Expert

Alumni Startup Award – Shubham Nandi, Founder of Rigbetel Labs

The evening concluded with a vote of thanks by Dr. Ipsita Swain, followed by a celebratory dinner at the poolside and exclusive passes to the SUNBURN event—adding a fun twist to the evening.

The Alumni Meet 2024 successfully reignited old friendships, fostered professional networks, and left everyone feeling proud and connected to their alma mater.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with an emphasis on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
