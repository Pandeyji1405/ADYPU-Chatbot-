Vastu Course

Your Journey into the Design Harmonious Spaces: Vastu Course

Discover the ancient science of Vastu Shastra, which aligns your living and working spaces with the natural elements to enhance well-being, prosperity, and harmony. This 6-month online course provides a thorough introduction to Vastu principles, offering insights into how spatial arrangements influence energy flow and balance. Ideal for architecture enthusiasts, interior designers, and anyone passionate about creating positive spaces, this course equips you with the skills to apply Vastu in daily life.

Duration: 6 Months

Eligibility :

12th from any stream (Arts/Commerce/Science)

Mode : Online

Program Highlights

Learn the principles of Vastu for residential, commercial, and personal spaces

Detailed study of energy flow and spatial arrangements

Practical applications with real-life case studies

Taught by experienced Vastu experts

Fundamentals of Vastu Shastra and its ancient origins

Designing harmonious homes, offices, and gardens

Do I need a background in architecture or design to join? No, the course is open to anyone with an interest in Vastu, regardless of background.

Can I apply Vastu principles to existing structures? Yes, the course teaches methods for Vastu corrections and improvements to enhance energy flow.

Will I receive certification upon course completion? Yes, a certificate will be awarded after successfully completing the course.
