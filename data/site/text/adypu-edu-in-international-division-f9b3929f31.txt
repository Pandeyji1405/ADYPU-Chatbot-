About International Division

The International Division at Ajeenkya D Y Patil University is a dedicated unit committed to enhancing the global presence and engagement of the university. Our core focus is to create and nurture international relationships that enrich the academic, cultural, and professional experiences of our students, faculty, researchers and staff. Through a diverse array of programs and services, we aim to foster a vibrant, inclusive, and globally connected campus community.

Established in 2018, with the aim to enhance the educational experience by offering diverse and comprehensive programs that promote global awareness and competence. The department expanded in 2022 and has since collaborated with 30 countries like USA, UK, Japan, Taiwan, Philippines, Malaysia, Dubai, Israel, Poland, Russia, South Africa, Spain, France, Belarus, Belgium, Australia and so on.

The International Division Brings Global Opportunities from Institutions, Government Organizations and Industries for all possible activities to engage the ADYPU Family members, especially on sustainable global goals.

To be a globally recognized leader in fostering cross-cultural understanding, academic excellence, and impactful collaborations that transcend borders, shaping future leaders equipped to address global challenges.

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant and inclusive international community within ADYPU, fostering a culture of global citizenship, academic innovation, and cross-cultural exchange. Through strategic partnerships, transformative learning experiences, and interdisciplinary collaboration, we empower students, faculty, and staff to thrive in an interconnected world, driving positive change and making meaningful contributions to society.

At Ajeenkya D Y Patil University (ADYPU), we are dedicated to enriching the academic journey, facilitating cultural integration, and ensuring the overall well-being of our international students. Our initiatives exemplify ADYPU’s commitment to cultivating a welcoming and supportive atmosphere, promoting academic excellence, personal growth, and cultural enrichment for international students throughout their tenure at the university. Additionally, we provide customized language support programs to help international students overcome language barriers and enhance their communication skills, and dedicated career counseling and job placement services to assist them in navigating the job market, securing internships, and achieving their professional goals post-graduation.

Highlights of International Student Initiatives

International Student Orientation: Orientation programs are tailored for international students, covering academic programs, campus facilities, accommodation, visa rules, cultural norms, and support services.

International Student Clubs and Organizations: ADYPU promotes student-led clubs for international students, fostering social networking, professional growth, and cultural enrichment.

Global Education Experiences: ADYPU provides study abroad programs, exchange opportunities, and internships, helping international students gain global perspectives and cross-cultural skills.

International Student Advisory Services: ADYPU’s International Division or student services offer specialized advisory support for international students.

ADYPU actively cultivates international collaborations with esteemed universities, research institutions, industry partners, and organizations globally. These collaborations enhance academic exchange, research partnerships, student mobility, and joint initiatives, promoting global learning experiences and advancing knowledge across diverse disciplines.

Academic Partnerships: The University collaborates with prestigious global universities and educational institutions to facilitate joint research initiatives. These partnerships offer students opportunities for study abroad programs, cross-cultural learning experiences, and exposure to diverse academic perspectives.

Research Collaborations: Collaborative research projects with international research institutions and organizations span various fields such as science, technology, engineering, arts, and humanities. These partnerships foster innovation, knowledge exchange, and interdisciplinary collaboration, driving advancements in global research and development.

Industry Partnerships: Partnerships with leading global corporations and industry leaders provide industry-aligned academic programs, internships, and industry-driven projects. These collaborations equip students with practical skills and real-world industry exposure.

International Conferences and Workshops: International conferences, workshops, and seminars are organized in collaboration with global partners. These events create platforms for scholars, researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to exchange ideas, share best practices, and collaborate on emerging trends and challenges in their fields.

Faculty Exchange and Visiting Scholar Programs: Faculty exchange and visiting scholar programs with partner universities enable faculty members to engage in collaborative research projects, deliver guest lectures, and participate in academic events and conferences abroad. These programs promote academic collaboration, professional development, and knowledge sharing among faculty from different institutions.

Overall, ADYPU’s international collaborations are crucial in nurturing global perspectives, academic excellence, and cross-cultural understanding among students, faculty, and staff. These partnerships align with the university’s mission to prepare future leaders and innovators to succeed in a rapidly changing global environment. By engaging in these global partnerships, ADYPU not only enhances its academic offerings but also fosters a vibrant, inclusive community that is well-equipped to address the challenges of the future.

The international admission process at Ajeenkya D Y Patil University (ADYPU) is designed to facilitate the enrolment of international students from diverse backgrounds for undergraduate, postgraduate, or doctoral programs. Here’s an overview:

Program Selection: International students should explore the range of programs offered, reviewing requirements, eligibility criteria, and deadlines to choose the most suitable program.

Online Application: Applicants must complete and submit an online application form on ADYPU’s official website, including personal information, educational background, and program preferences.

Document Submission: Supporting documents such as academic transcripts, standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, GRE, GMAT), English proficiency scores (TOEFL, IELTS), letters of recommendation, a statement of purpose, and a passport copy must be submitted.

Evaluation and Admission Decision: The admissions committee reviews application materials to assess academic qualifications, English proficiency, and overall suitability. Decisions are based on academic performance, test scores, recommendations, and the statement of purpose.

Offer of Admission: Selected applicants receive an official offer letter outlining terms of admission, program details, tuition fees, and any scholarships or financial aid awarded.

Visa Application: Admitted students must obtain a student visa or study permit to study in India.

Pre-arrival Orientation: ADYPU offers pre-arrival orientation sessions covering academic expectations, campus facilities, accommodation, visa requirements, cultural norms, and student support services.

Arrival and Enrolment: Upon arrival, students’ complete enrolment, attend orientation sessions, register for classes, and obtain student ID cards. ADYPU’s international student services provide support to help students adjust to university life.
