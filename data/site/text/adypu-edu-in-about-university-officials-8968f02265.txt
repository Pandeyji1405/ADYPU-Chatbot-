Dr. Sudhakar Shinde is a Metallurgical Engineer from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology Nagpur and has done his Masters in Personnel Management & Industrial Relations from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. He has chosen to be in the educational field and his core competencies is in Higher Educational Institute/University Administration.

His expertise in managing establishment of new institutes and administration of KG to PG institutes like Engineering, Medical, Dental, and Polytechnic and schools and so on…

He has worked with a repute institutions such as Datta Meghe College of Engineering, Sharad Pawar Dental College & Hospital, NMIMS University, Mumbai, NHIMS , Aruna Manharlal Shah Institute of Management Studies & Research, Ghatkopar, Durgadevi Saraf Institute of Management Studies Malad Mumbai, Datta Meghe Institute of Higher Education and Research (Deemed University) Wardha. His previous association was with Symbiosis Skills and Professional University Pune.

He has a rich experience of nearly 25 years as an Administrator and is well versed with recent educational systems. He had worked abroad in UAE and West Africa at Ghana. He is well versed with the regulatory norms of AICTE/UGC/DTE/NIRF/NAAC. He is known as excellent administrator, team leader and motivator.

Email: aparna.mhetras@adypu.edu.in

Prof Aparna Rahul Mhetras, an Environmental architect and IGBC accredited faculty, brings 23 years of professional and 11 years of teaching experience. She earned her B.Arch from Shivaji University, Kolhapur, and her M.Arch in 2015 from Pune University. A Project Architect for award-winning projects like Ruby Hall Clinic, she co-manages Maya Design Studio in Pune. A dedicated researcher in Environmental Psychology and the History of Architecture, she has presented papers at conferences and is a research scholar at Nirma University, Ahmedabad. She is known for her effective teaching, administration, and research skills, building strong connections with her students.

Email: sunny.thomas@adypu.edu.in

Dr. Sunny Thomas , BA, MA, BPh, LLB (Spl), LLM, PhD , NET is currently the Dean of School of Liberal Arts and School of Law. He was also the former Dean for Faculty of Arts at CVM University, Gujarat and former Principal, Institute of Language Studies and Applied Social Sciences. He possesses a total teaching experience of 29 years and a total administrative experience of 13 Years. Dr. Thomas has obtained 1 Patent and has published 21 Research Papers and 2 Books.

Email: rajaram.golgire@adypu.edu.in

Prof Rajaram T. Golgire, a seasoned architect, is a recipient of the Best Practicing Fast Track Award from the Pune Municipal Corporation. With over 45 successfully completed projects in Pune, encompassing residential, commercial, educational, and industrial domains, he is also an accomplished author of two Marathi books. Distinguished by memberships in IIA, COA, AICTE, NCERT, and FIV, he actively contributes as a panel member and examiner for prestigious universities including Pune University, Bharati Vidyapeeth, and Shivaji University.

Email: sachin.rajas@adypu.edu.in

Dr Sachin Manoharrao Rajas, with M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Mathematics from Amravati University, India, boasts 22 years of teaching experience across four universities. His expertise lies in geometric function theory and fractional calculus, particularly in Univalent and Multivalent functions. With a significant contribution of 12 international research papers, 7 conference presentations, and involvement in 2 engineering mathematics books, he serves as a reviewer for Tata Mc-Graw Hill Ltd. Dr. Rajas, a distinguished member of multiple academic associations, significantly contributed to curriculum design at MIT’s AOE and GHRCEM in Pune.His transition to ADYPU reflects his dedication to innovation, advanced curriculum development, and fostering a corporate culture.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with an emphasis on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
