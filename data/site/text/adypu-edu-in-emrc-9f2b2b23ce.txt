Academic Vision

The Educational Media Research Center (EMRC) at Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is dedicated to the creation, research, and dissemination of high-quality educational media to enhance teaching and learning experiences across disciplines. The center serves as a hub for innovative audio-visual content development, fostering media literacy, academic research, and technological integration in education.

EMRC plays a pivotal role in supporting curriculum delivery through the production of instructional videos, documentaries, e-learning modules, and interactive media content. Equipped with modern production studios, editing suites, and expert staff, the center provides hands-on training to students and faculty, encouraging the use of media as a powerful tool for communication and education.

In addition to content creation, the center actively engages in research on the effectiveness of educational media, pedagogical innovations, and media impact assessment. It also collaborates with academic departments, industry partners, and external institutions to develop projects that bridge the gap between media and education.

Key Activities:

Audio-visual content development for academic subjects

Research on educational media effectiveness

Training programs in media production and communication

Workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects

Support for student and faculty media initiatives

Through EMRC, ADYPU reaffirms its commitment to blending creativity, technology, and pedagogy—preparing students to thrive in a media-rich, knowledge-driven world.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with an emphasis on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
