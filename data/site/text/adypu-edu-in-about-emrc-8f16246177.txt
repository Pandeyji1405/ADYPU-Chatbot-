About ADYPU - EMRC

The Educational Media Research Center (EMRC) at Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is a state-of-the-art facility committed to the advancement of educational communication through media innovation, production, and research. Established with the vision to integrate technology and pedagogy, EMRC supports the university’s mission of delivering impactful, engaging, and accessible education across disciplines.

To become a leading center of excellence in educational media, empowering learners and educators through cutting-edge audio-visual content, research, and skill development.

To produce high-quality educational media that supports teaching, learning, and outreach.

To conduct research on the use of media in education and assess its impact on learner engagement and outcomes.

To train students and faculty in media literacy, production techniques, and educational communication.

To foster collaborations with academic institutions, media professionals, and industry stakeholders.

Core Functions and Activities

1. Media Production for Education:

EMRC develops curriculum-based audio-visual content including:

Video lectures, documentaries, and explainer videos

E-learning modules and interactive digital content

Awareness films and promotional media for educational campaigns All content is designed to meet academic standards and pedagogical goals, with strong visual and narrative quality.

2. Research and Innovation:

The center undertakes interdisciplinary research on:

Effectiveness of media in teaching and learning

Student engagement through visual learning strategies

Integration of emerging technologies (AR/VR, animation, interactive media) in education

Findings from these studies contribute to the development of best practices in educational media.

3. Training and Capacity Building:

EMRC conducts regular training sessions and hands-on workshops for:

Faculty on content creation, camera handling, and instructional design

Students in video editing, scripting, direction, and digital storytelling

Media production skills as part of academic programs (e.g., Certificate and Diploma in Photography & Audio-visual Editing)

4. Infrastructure and Facilities:

The center houses:

Professional video recording studios with green screen setup

Non-linear editing (NLE) workstations with advanced software

Audio recording booth and sound editing facilities

Equipment including HD cameras, lighting kits, drones, and audio gear

5. Collaborative and Outreach Initiatives:

Joint projects with departments and external institutions

Production of content for MOOCs and educational broadcasting

Industry interface for internships, live projects, and content co-creation

Media support for university events, conferences, and academic outreach

Academic Integration

EMRC actively supports the academic framework by offering inputs in:

Curriculum development for media and communication programs

Internship and project opportunities for students in creative domains

Guest lectures and masterclasses by industry experts

The center also hosts Certificate and Diploma Programs in Photography and Audio-visual Editing, with an intake capacity of 60 seats per course. These programs emphasize experiential learning, practical skill development, and portfolio building, making graduates industry-ready.

Impact and Future Goals:

EMRC has contributed significantly to enriching the learning experience at ADYPU through its creative and research-driven approach. Moving forward, the center aims to:

Launch more interdisciplinary media projects

Expand digital content for remote and inclusive learning

Collaborate nationally and internationally to share knowledge and best practices

Introduce innovation labs focused on AR/VR, AI in media, and immersive storytelling.
