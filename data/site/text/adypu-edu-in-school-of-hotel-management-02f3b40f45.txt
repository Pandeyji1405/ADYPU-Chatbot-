Internship & Industry

We believe that the key to preparing our students for successful careers in the dynamic hospitality industry is to provide them with practical, real-world experience. Through a wide network of industry partnerships, our students embark on immersive internships in prestigious hotels, restaurants, and event management companies, gaining hands-on experience and insights that set them apart in the job market.

The School of Hospitality and Hotel Administration is dedicated to not only providing top-tier education but also ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared for thriving careers in the dynamic hospitality industry. Our commitment to excellence extends to our robust placement assistance and diverse career opportunities.

Diverse Career Pathways

Strong Industry Ties: We have established deep connections with leading hotels, resorts, restaurants, and hospitality organizations worldwide. These relationships open doors to internships, co-op programs, and exclusive job placements for our students.

Experiential Learning: Practical experience is at the heart of our curriculum. From culinary labs to hotel simulations, our students engage in hands-on learning, ensuring they are industry-ready from day one.

Global Exposure: In today’s globalized world, a global perspective is essential. Our international programs, multicultural student body, and exposure to global hospitality trends equip our graduates with the skills and adaptability to excel in diverse settings.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship: We encourage a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship, empowering students to think creatively, identify opportunities, and shape the future of hospitality.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: we take immense pride in our commitment to providing students with state-of-the-art facilities that stand as a testament to our dedication to pioneering hospitality excellence. Our campus is equipped with cutting-edge kitchens, fully equipped restaurants, and simulated hotel environments that closely mirror real-world settings. These facilities provide our students with a unique opportunity to hone their skills and gain hands-on experience.

Guest Lectures and Industry Events: We proudly embrace a core feature that sets us apart – the dynamic inclusion of guest lectures and industry events. We believe in bridging the gap between academic learning and practical industry insights. Renowned experts, successful professionals, and influential figures in the field of hospitality frequently grace our campus with their presence, offering our students a first hand look at the real-world challenges and opportunities within the industry.
