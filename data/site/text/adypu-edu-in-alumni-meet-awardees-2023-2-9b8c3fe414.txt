Alumni Meet 2023

Alumni Meet 2023 – Celebrating Connections, Fostering Community

The Alumni Meet 2023 was held on 3rd March 2023 at the Main Auditorium of ADYPU’s Dental Building, bringing together graduates, faculty, and students for an evening filled with reconnection and inspiration. With over 230 attendees, the event was a testament to the growing strength and unity of the ADYPU alumni network.

The event commenced with a warm welcome by Janhavi Gandhe, Vice-President of the Student Council, followed by a ceremonial Saraswati Vandana and Diya lighting by Hon’ble Vice Chancellor Prof. Hridaysh Deshpande. His address emphasized the value of maintaining strong alumni ties and assured continued support from the university.

Ms. Priya Abraham, Head of Student Services, and Dr. Ipsita Swain, In-charge of the Alumni Association, shared insights on how regular alumni engagement fosters professional growth and lasting friendships. The highlight of the evening was the alumni testimonial session, where notable alumni including Pranshu Tople, Shubham Nandi, and Gokul Menon reflected on their journeys and credited the university for shaping their success.

The event concluded with a vote of thanks by Dr. Ipsita Swain, followed by a poolside dinner and SUNBURN passes gifted to alumni, making the evening both nostalgic and festive.

The Alumni Meet 2023 reaffirmed ADYPU’s commitment to nurturing a strong and supportive alumni community, and laid the foundation for many such reunions in the future.

We are a world-class university that prepares students for the global market, with an emphasis on fostering growth through a spirit of innovation.
