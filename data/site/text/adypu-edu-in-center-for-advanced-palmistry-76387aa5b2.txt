Your Journey into the Unveil Life’s Secrets Through Your Hands: Palmistry Course

Embark on a journey to understand the art of palmistry, where the lines and shapes of your hands reveal profound insights into personality, health, and destiny. This course offers a structured approach to mastering palm reading, blending ancient traditions with modern interpretations. Whether you’re a curious beginner or looking to enhance your metaphysical skills, this online course is perfect for anyone with a 12th qualification. Learn how to interpret the secrets your palms hold.

Duration: 6 Months

Eligibility :

12th from any stream (Arts/Commerce/Science)

Mode : Online

Program Highlights

Learn to read major and minor lines, mounts, and hand shapes

Explore personality, health, and career predictions through palms

Taught by experienced palmistry practitioners

Flexible learning with real-world examples and case studies

What Will You Explore?:

The significance of major and minor lines in palmistry

Finger shapes, mounts, and hand types for personality analysis

Predicting health, relationships, and career through palm reading

Historical roots and cultural interpretations of palmistry

Is prior knowledge of palmistry required? No, this course is designed for beginners and those with an interest in metaphysical sciences.
