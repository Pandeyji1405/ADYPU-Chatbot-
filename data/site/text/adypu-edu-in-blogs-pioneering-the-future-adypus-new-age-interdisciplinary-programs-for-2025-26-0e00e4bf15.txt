Pioneering the Future: ADYPU’s New-Age Interdisciplinary Programs for 2025-26

June 20, 2025

In a world where industries are increasingly interconnected, the future belongs to those who can think beyond silos. Recognizing this shift, Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is set to roll out a series of trailblazing interdisciplinary programs in the academic year 2025-2026. These courses are not just degrees—they’re designed to shape well-rounded professionals equipped for the hybrid careers of tomorrow.

Let’s dive into what makes these programs unique and how they are redefining the boundaries between disciplines, industries, and innovation.

1. Where Finance Meets Technology: B.Tech FinTech and BBA FinTech

The fusion of finance and technology is more than a trend—it’s a revolution. ADYPU introduces:

B.Tech FinTech (in collaboration with Zell & Paytm)

BBA FinTech (in collaboration with Zell & Deloitte)

Both programs blend deep financial acumen with technological fluency. Students gain hands-on experience in blockchain, data analytics, financial modeling, and app development.

These industry partnerships ensure students don’t just learn theory—they apply it to real-world scenarios. With leaders like Paytm and Deloitte backing the curriculum, graduates will be job-ready for roles in digital payments, investment tech, banking solutions, and financial software development.

2. Shaping Financial Leaders: MBA in Applied Finance

Finance is no longer just about numbers—it’s about insights, agility, and innovation.

The MBA in Applied Finance (with Zell & Deloitte) is curated for future-ready finance professionals. It combines:

Strategic financial planning

Regulatory compliance know-how

Practical application through case studies and simulations

With mentorship from industry experts and access to real-time tools, this program prepares graduates for high-impact roles in investment banking, risk analysis, corporate finance, and more.

3. Engineering Excellence: B.Tech Civil Engineering with L&T

Infrastructure is the backbone of national development. ADYPU’s B.Tech in Civil Engineering (with L&T) offers a curriculum that’s as strong as the structures students will help build.

Highlights include:

Real-time project exposure with L&T’s infrastructure projects

Emphasis on sustainable engineering and smart construction

On-ground internships and immersive site visits

This partnership ensures that students aren’t just trained in labs but shaped on real construction sites with one of India’s most respected engineering giants.

4. Understanding Law with Business: BBA LLB

In today’s regulatory-heavy landscape, professionals who understand both law and business are invaluable.

The BBA LLB program at ADYPU integrates core management principles with an in-depth understanding of legal frameworks. Key features include:

Cross-disciplinary modules on corporate law, business ethics, and compliance

Moot courts and internships with law firms

Focus on emerging legal areas like cyber law, intellectual property, and international trade law

Graduates will be adept at navigating both boardrooms and courtrooms, making them ideal for careers in corporate law, legal consultancy, and policy-making.

Why These Courses Matter

Industry Relevance: Every program is designed with direct input from industry leaders like Paytm, Deloitte, L&T, and Zell.Experiential Learning: Live projects, internships, and simulations are core to each course.

Holistic Development: These are not single-skill degrees. They build thinkers, problem-solvers, and innovators.

These interdisciplinary programs break the traditional walls between fields. A techie can now understand finance. A business graduate can decode legal contracts. An engineer can speak the language of sustainability. That’s the kind of future ADYPU is building.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Are these programs open to international students? Yes, ADYPU welcomes students from across the globe. All programs are aligned with global academic standards.

Q2. Will students get placement assistance? Absolutely. ADYPU’s dedicated career services cell offers training, internships, and placement support in partnership with leading companies.

Q3. Can students pursue higher education abroad after completing these programs? Yes. The programs are recognized and structured to support further education, both in India and internationally.

Final Thoughts

In a fast-evolving world, staying ahead means embracing convergence—of ideas, industries, and disciplines. ADYPU’s new programs for 2025-26 are a bold step in that direction. Whether you dream of leading a financial startup, managing megastructures, or shaping legal policies, these courses offer the perfect launchpad.

Explore a future where boundaries blur—and possibilities expand.
