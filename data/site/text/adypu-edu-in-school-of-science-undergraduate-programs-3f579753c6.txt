Programs Offered

The School of Science at Ajeenkya DY Patil University offers a dynamic and future-ready Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Physics program. Designed to meet the demands of the 21st-century scientific landscape, the curriculum combines strong theoretical foundations with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies. Students are exposed to a wide array of interdisciplinary applications and trained in computational tools, research methodologies, and industry-relevant soft skills, ensuring holistic scientific development.
