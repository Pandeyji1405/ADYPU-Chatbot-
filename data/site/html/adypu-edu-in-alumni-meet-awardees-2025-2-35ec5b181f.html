Alumni Meet 2025

The ADYPU Alumni Association is a vibrant and growing community that celebrates the legacy and achievements of Ajeenkya DY Patil University graduates. Relaunched with renewed vision during the Alumni Meet 2025, the association is committed to fostering lifelong connections, mentorship, and professional growth.

Held on April 19, 2025, the Alumni Meet brought together over 80 alumni and university members at the Chairman’s Lawn Area. The event featured an inspiring address by the Hon’ble Vice Chancellor and Deans, an Alumni Awards Ceremony honoring distinguished alumni across categories like excellence, innovation, startups, and global achievement, and a lively ice-breaker session led by Ms. Gayatri Mahulikar.

One of the key highlights was the official announcement of new Alumni Association members, reinforcing a shared commitment to collaboration and community building. The evening concluded with memory-sharing sessions, rekindling old bonds and igniting new possibilities for alumni involvement.

The ADYPU Alumni Association continues to grow as a platform of pride, progress, and connection—inviting all past graduates to stay involved and shape the future together.
