ADYPU: Where EveryStudent Thrives Equally

ADYPU offers exceptional opportunities for fresh graduates to make the most of the booming job market. We invite companies offering high packages on campus than ever before so that all our students with unique talents get opportunities to appear for the on-campus placements drive. This year, the average Cost to Company (CTC) for fresh graduates has increased to 6.5 LPA, and over 500 companies have participated in campus recruitment drives. This upward trend is a testament to the growing demand for skilled professionals and the recognition of the value of higher education.

At ADYPU, we are committed to not only imparting world-class education but also ensuring that our graduates are fully equipped to navigate the dynamic corporate landscape. Our Placement and Corporate Relations team works tirelessly in collaboration with course mentors and faculty members to meticulously evaluate each student’s placement readiness based on industry-recommended parameters.

Needs Assessment: Evaluating industry demands and trends to identify key skills required for successful placements.

Skill Development: Designing tailored training programs to enhance students’ competencies in alignment with industry standards.

Personalized Guidance: Providing individualized counseling sessions and mentorship to prepare students for the recruitment process.

Industry Interaction: Facilitating regular interactions with industry experts and recruiters to provide insights into industry expectations.

Placement Evaluation: Assessing students’ readiness for placement through mock interviews, aptitude tests, and group discussions.

Placement Assistance: Actively liaising with companies to arrange placement opportunities and facilitating the recruitment process.

Post-Placement Support: Offering mentorship programs and feedback mechanisms to ensure students’ seamless transition into the corporate world.

Pre-placement talks are an essential part of the campus recruitment process, providing students with valuable insights into the corporate world and potential career paths. These interactive sessions are conducted by recruiters who share their expertise and experiences, helping students understand the company’s culture, work environment, and expectations.
