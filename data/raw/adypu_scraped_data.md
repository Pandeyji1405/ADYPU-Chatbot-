## About ADYPU

Ajeenkya DY Patil University (ADYPU) is an innovation-focused institution in Pune offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

### Academic Schools
Engineering, Management, Design, Hotel Management, Law, Liberal Arts, Architecture, Film and Media, Science, Allied Health Sciences, Online and Distance Education.

### Key Features
- T-Shaped Professional curriculum combining specialized expertise with interdisciplinary knowledge
- Global partnerships with universities in the US, UK, and Europe for immersion programs
- Innovation centers: DRIF (Design, Research and Innovation Foundation) and Entrepreneurship and Innovation Centre
